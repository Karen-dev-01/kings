Our Facilities

  • Science Laboratory

    Our school laboratory is well-equipped with modern tools and materials that make learning practical and exciting. It provides students with hands-on experience in science experiments, helping them understand concepts better and develop a love for discovery.

  • Art and Music Room

    Our Art and Music Room is a creative space where students express their imagination and talent through drawing, painting, singing, and playing instruments. It is well-equipped with art materials and musical instruments to help learners explore their artistic potential.

  • Books & Library

    Our school library is a quiet and welcoming space where students discover the joy of reading and learning. It is well-stocked with textbooks, storybooks, reference materials, and educational resources that support both academic study and personal growth.

Meet Our teachers

At KCC, we believe that great teachers make great schools. Our team of educators is made up of experienced, well-trained, and passionate professionals who are committed to bringing out the best in every child. Each teacher combines academic expertise with a genuine love for teaching, ensuring that lessons are engaging, practical, and easy to understand. Beyond academics, our teachers serve as mentors and role models, guiding students in values such as discipline, integrity, and teamwork. Together, they create a supportive and stimulating learning environment where every learner feels valued and inspired to achieve excellence.
